WE ARE FITGEAR

Premium essentials shouldn't be a luxury. At FITGEAR, we traveled across the globe in search of industry experts with decades of experience.

The growth of FITGEAR marks an important role as an industry pioneer. Our approach in this business is distinct. Adapting our business model makes it possible for us to share with you the world’s finest quality, at fair and honest prices.

Our structure allows us to communicate with customers directly. It is rather unique from your typical apparel companies. We minimize outsourcing as much as we can and instead, do most of the back-end and front-end tasks in-house, removing any unnecessary procedures that will increase costs.

By executing our designs in-house, selling online, eliminating the intermediaries and working directly with our partner manufacturers and factories, we’re able to cut a huge amount of non-essential costs.

We cut as much unnecessary costs as we can, making the finest fabrics activewear to you at the best possible price.




FITGEAR was freshly founded in 2016 by a group of fabrics experts with a simple idea. Sportswear is not a luxury. Everyone deserves the best suit whenever we need it. Today, we're empowering sport's lovers across the country with every order! We specialize in Dri Fit and QuickDry fabric industry.



QUALITY

We spent more than 16 months on research and development, sourcing for the highest quality materials, maximising performance features. Our products go through extensive tests to ensure that they are durable to last a lifetime. We want our essentials to be your new best friend.

FIT

We’ve surveyed over 3000+ body types to be able to perfect the fit of our products to suit all body types. We make it easy for you to exchange so that you can find your perfect size hassle free. Our priority is how you look and feel in our products because we believe that greatness starts from within.

PURPOSE

Every detail and stitch has a purpose to maximize comfort and performance. We want to challenge the way how essentials are made in the industry so rest assured that you’re only getting the best of the best.

HONEST

We believe in fair pricing without compromising quality. This is why we are only able to release 1 product at a time & we will need YOUR support to fund for our next release as we spend 80% of our profits on research & development.

WHY PRE-ORDER?

With most of our funds going into creating the materials, patenting designs & new technology, we want to remove unnecessary costs to ensure that we can offer these products at prices that are accessible by many.

WHAT ARE THE UNNECESSARY COSTS?

Storage cost, production cost and also overhead costs that are involved with managing inventory. Saving on these costs help us to give the value back to the customers so ultimately, we are trading money with time.

WHY DOES IT TAKE SO LONG?

MATERIALS

Each component of our products are made in different parts of the world. Materials from Germany, thread from Hong Kong, and so on. This means all these materials will have to be shipped to our production plant in order for the production to start.

WORKMANSHIP

Working with a factory with 17 years of experience manufacturing for international brands, we are withholding the highest standards possible. We have a return rate of 0.13% as defects are minimal, and a 98% customer satisfaction rate.
