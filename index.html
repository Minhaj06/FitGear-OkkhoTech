WHY FITGEAR

INSTANT COOLING

Our Cooling fabrics help maintain a comfortable body temperature during physical performance and hot weather. By using a special Cotton Blend Yarn we are able to quickly transport body heat to the outside while increasing the breathability and ventilation of our fabrics.

SILKY SOFT COTTON BLEND

Our Cotton Blend uses a special piqué knit technique that soften the surface of the fabric, makes it as soft to wear and comfortable to the max.

6% ELASTANE

With this added 6% Elastane, it provides a perfect combination of smoothness and stretchability, you don't want any restrictions during your favourite sports!

SUSTAINABLE STRETCH

Our fabric withstands a long term stretch test and therefore can retain its original shape even after 1,000 washes. Can be washed and dried at home in the washing machine (or in a hotel sink).
